Our Story

Jonathan's Story:

I was always a romantic but never had anyone to really show myself to, or rather, no one cared. Then a mutual friend introduced us. The late night talks, and with the pandemic all we could only have video dates. We watched so many shows together. I wasn't falling in love with a girl, I was falling in love with my best friend. The world that seemed dark now filled with beautiful God's rays, the world that seemed monochrome, now had so much vibrant color to it. She is my muse, my player 2, my angel of light. Quoting Coldplay's "My mirror, my sword and shield".

Daisy's Story:

We met via friends, started talking over text. Having hours long video dates, talking about our future. I fell in love with your voice and the funny jokes you would tell me. I love the way you talk about your love of art. I know I'm marrying my best friend, someone I love with my whole heart and soul.
